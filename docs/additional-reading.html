Section 6 Additional reading

The UCLA Institute for Digital Research and Education provide usefull ressources on R in general (https://stats.idre.ucla.edu/r/), as well as information and practical examples on logistic regression (https://stats.idre.ucla.edu/r/dae/logit-regression/), and mixed effects logistic regression (https://stats.idre.ucla.edu/r/dae/mixed-effects-logistic-regression/).

R bloggers (https://www.r-bloggers.com) is also a usefull ressource for R programming at different skill levels.

Finally, by typing in the analysis you wish to conduct or the problem you are encoutering followed by R or [R] in a search engine you will most likely find usefull comment threads on sites such as Stack Overflow (https://stackoverflow.com/) and Cross-validated (https://stats.stackexchange.com/).
